Our history

Our bakery is run by our family and has been operating at this location since 1991. Our recipes come from our grandmother as well as from our younger generations. We are a blend of tradition and modern times.

Ingridients

All our products are vegan and 100% verified.

In case you suffer from certain diseases and you have to pay special attention to your diet, you do not have to worry about our products. If you want more information about the origin of food, certificates and permits, feel free to contact us.

Black Forest Cake

Black Forest gâteau or Black Forest cake is a chocolate sponge cake with a rich cherry filling based on the German dessert Schwarzwälder Kirschtorte. Typically, Black Forest gateau consists of several layers of chocolate sponge cake sandwiched with vegan whipped cream and cherries.

Raspberry Cake

The classic of our childhood: raspberry cake, or as we like to call it in German "Himbeerschnitte". Our recipe is made with a fluffy sponge cake base, a layer of chilled vegan yogurt and cream, and fresh raspberries on top.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Our cookies are made of vegan chocolate chips, vegan margarine, and egg substitutes. A chocolate chocolate chip cookie uses a dough flavored with chocolate or cocoa powder, before chocolate chips are mixed in. When you prefer ‘double’ or ‘triple’ chocolate chip cookies, we do have those as well.
